Ryan Garner

Software Engineer, with a hunger to learn and build applications that make a positive impact. On the forefront of innovation; building effective, creative, and forward thinking products, I do this by partnering with others to solve complex problems, pioneer undiscovered possibilities and seeking to be a continual student.

I may have been late to the game but I found my passion! I always thought my dream was to be a Commercial Project Manager, watching, building and contributing to a cities legacy was a rewarding feeling. The grind, the teamwork the drive to push a project across the finish line. It seemed like an appropriate dream given my background in Construction Management at Colorado State University. I gravitated toward superintendent and project management positions and even started my own General Contraction company for a while (Note to self don't start a business on the same day a global pandemic goes into effect). Still something seemed to be missing, I yearned to learn more, yearned to learn something different, to... build something different. It wasn't gradual it was sudden like an African desert storm; the spark I had been looking for when I signed up for my first free online Javascript course and 2 hours turned into 12. Since then, I've been hooked and I'm proud of it. Full-stack web development encompasses all my passions; it incorporates creativity, team building, and complex problem solving. I fell in love not only when software development but the community of inspiring, giving individuals I've met along that way! My specialties include quickly learning new skills and programming languages, problem solving, team collaboration and project management. So far I have SQL, JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, GraphQL, Vue, NodeJS, Python, MongoDB and Git/GitHub under my belt. I've started learning Angular, Ruby on Rails, and Kotlin.
